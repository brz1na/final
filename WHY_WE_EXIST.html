WE WANT ANIMALS
TO LIVE HAPPILY.

The Hellermann family loves dogs. Patric's uncle Klaus has been a certified dog trainer competing internationally with his German shepherds since the early 1970s. Nina is an active proponent of PETA and a supporter of Paula's choice. Since 2005 Patric and Nina have banned products tested on animals from their family household. Patric and Nina initiate projects with local animal shelters. Why this matters? Because we act. We commit. 3% of our revenues go to local animal protection projects. No exceptions, no excuses.
