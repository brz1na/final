FINEST TECHNIQUES

Outstanding details

  • ROUNDED COMB JOINTS

    "My favorite wooden joint is a dense comb. A lasting construction. Like no other joint, it emphasizes the beauty of solid wood. It shows the wood's head without detracting.The EVERCHEST sports up to 412 comb joints."

    Patric Hellermann

  • SANDING

    Our craftsmen hand-sand each and every part. Before they are assembled, and after they are assembled. The result is an almost impossible precision and fitting of all parts, and flawless edges and surfaces.

  • HANDBUILT FRAMES

    "We build our watch slots from solid wood by hand. Patric was obsessed with this detail from the start. It is one of the most complicated tasks, arranging the sides and the top rim perfectly. And it was worth it."

    Nina Hellermann

  • FINISHING

    Our massive woods receive the best attention.
    Each part is hand-painted with 3-5 layers to give it a perfect finish. Ready to shine in every light.

  • SANDWICH VEINS

    "Beauty is in contrasts. I enjoy the unexpected.
    In our designs I pay attention to contrasts. The EVERCHEST contains layers of solid wood from other species. Our "sandwich veins", derived from the dials in some of my watches."

    Patric Hellermann

  • FITTING GRAINS

    The woods we use have grains full of character. None is like the other. We ensure the perfect match. For that we build each product from the same wood slab. Our craftsmen accomplish outstanding fits by carefully picking the right parts.

  • MARQUETRY

    Paper thin solid wood slices impressed in the top. This is when a master craftsman becomes a true artist. His tools are a scalpel and a tweezer. Only our veterans with decades of experience get to hand-craft your marquetry.

  • STITCHING

    Our Alcantara upholstery deserves special finish. We give it that with the careful stitching. Another thing to enjoy for many years to come.

GOOD FOR EVERYONE.

Responsible sourcing.

We source our woods with FSC secured sustainability. FSC, the Forest Stewardship Council, is a global forest certification system. It is considered the most influential initiative to ensure sustainable foresting and wood harvesting.

No MDF. No treated cardboard. No leather. Simply non-toxic. We breathe sustainability. No compromise. Forever joy.
